# Amaravati-Smart-CIty-Project-
Amaravati Smart City Development: A Multidisciplinary Sustainable Urban Planning Initiative by a Team of 14 IIT Tirupati Students

Amaravati, the planned capital city of Andhra Pradesh, is envisioned as a sustainable and people-centric smart city
The project aimed to identify high-growth urban zones and propose sustainable, technology-driven solutions across key domains including urban planning, transportation, water resource management, power infrastructure, biodiversity conservation, and waste management. The team developed a high-fidelity 3D city model and formulated strategies to support Amaravati's vision of becoming a people-centric, sustainable, and smart capital city.

Project Domains:
City Planning & 3D Modelling
Water Resource Management
Transport & Accessibility
Power Management
Biodiversity & Waste Management
